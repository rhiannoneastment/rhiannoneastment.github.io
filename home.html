What We Do

Research Themes

Our lab takes an integrative approach to ecology and evolutionary biology, combining field work, laboratory experiments, and computational modelling.

🦋

Behavioural Ecology

How animals make decisions under uncertainty — foraging, mate choice, predator avoidance — and how these behaviours evolve across populations.

🌡️

Climate Adaptation

Physiological and morphological responses to rapid environmental change, including range shifts, phenological mismatches, and thermal tolerance.

🔬

Community Ecology

The assembly rules, interaction networks, and biodiversity dynamics of ecological communities, from microbiomes to vertebrate assemblages.
